ArcGIS Monitor 2022

Project Info

ArcGIS Monitor collects data and information on the status, usage, availability, and resource utilization in ArcGIS enterprise GIS deployment. It also provides alerts, email notifications, performance statistics, and reports for monitored systems and sites. ArcGIS Monitor delivers value to administrators by proactively detecting issues and providing quantifiable operational metrics and usage trends over time. For managers, ArcGIS Monitor reduces administration costs and increases communication between GIS and IT staff, as well as senior management.

Since end of 2019, I have been leading the UX/UI redesign of ArcGIS Monitor 2022, partner with the product stakeholders on review and redesign the existing workflows and UX patterns. The 2022 version is comming soon and the BETA version has been announced with ESRI EAP(Early Adopter Program).
