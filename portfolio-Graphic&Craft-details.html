Project Info

This is a series of 2D/3D graphic work I did over the years, either as a course work, a casual design from my hobby, or as a volunteer work.

Thonet Chair Restore

I was lucky to get a pair of Thonet Sweetheart No. 16 Chairs Bentwood Chairs by Thonet & Zpm Radomsko. There came from straight from Poland and still have the labels attached! However, They came to me in pretty bad shape so I had to replace the cane webbing in the seats. I Spent more than 20 hours to finish one re-caning work, and it turned out beautiful!

Buoyancy

This is a course project while I was having my Master program in the Mississippi State University. The topic is about using digital tools to define a word, “buoyancy”, in 2D and 3D.

In 2D, I came up the idea of converting an Beijing opera face pattern into raster layers based on their color tones. Layers with different thickness cast various depth of shadows.

In 3D, got inspired by the floating ice in water, I made two 3D spatial objects: cubes of different densities floating in the waves in different depth, and an implied cube in an abstracted cube space.




Seal Carving

I started the quiet hobby - seal carving since childhood. I appreciate the loneliness and interaction with stone and carving knife.
