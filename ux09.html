ACTNOW - Afterschool for Children and Teens

  • 2017
  • User Experience Design, User Interface Design

Project Info

The ACTNOW Coalition works towards ensuring young people in Illinois have access to quality, affordable afterschool and youth development programs. The ACT Now Illinois Afterschool Program Mapping Database is a publicly viewable map of afterschool programs in Illinois with an administrative backend for maintaining the list of NPOs.
