Valeo Web App Design

Project Info

Valeo is a complex online financial application and database management system for the financial advisory firm located at Carmel, Indiana. The web app is used to help financial advisors manage their clients and assets, such as investment, insurance, banking accounts, estate properties and etc.

What I did

  • UX &UI Design
  • Form Design
  • Graphic/Icon Design
  • Branding and Design System

Valeo Dashboard Page

Project Background

This was my very first project after joining Inverse-Square, an Indianapolis based start-up software company. Before I joined, the Valeo project was completed the majority of development; however, the UX/UI part did not meet the client's expectations.

During the whole design review, iteration and development process, I had led and managed all the design related issues. My involvement was a huge uplifting and the product was sucessfully delivered after all UX/UI changes applied.

Low-fi Wireframes

The original designs were created using balsmiq for design communication purpose, which look busy with lots of information and in a low fidelity format. The client was overwhelmed by the low-difelity wireframe and expected to see high-fidelity mockup that could reveal a realistic outcome with UX/UI improvements.
