Valeo Web App Design

Project Info

Valeo is a complex online financial application and database management system for the financial advisory firm located at Carmel, Indiana. The web app is used to help financial advisors manage their clients and assets, such as investment, insurance, banking accounts, estate properties and etc.

What I did

  • UX &UI Design
  • Form Design
  • Graphic/Icon Design
  • Branding and Design System

Valeo Dashboard Page

Project Background

This is my very first project after I join Inverse-Square, an Indianapolis based start-up software company. My involvement was to review and uplift the user interface originally proposed by the team, which did not satisfy client expectations.

During the whole design review, iteration and development process, I have been leading and coordinating on any design related issues. The product was sucessfully delivered and made our customer happy.

Low-fi Wireframes

The original designs were created using balsmiq for design communication purpose, which look busy with loads of information and in a low fidelity format. The client was overwhelmed by the low-difelity wireframe and expected to see high-fidelity mockup that can reveal a realistic outcome with UX improvements.
